Elementals

How to Play

Playing Elementals is just like the classic game Rock, Paper, Scissors! To start, click on one of the elements to begin! When you click one one of the buttons, your choice will display on the bottom of the screen. After that the computer will pick an element and then the elements battle it out to see who wins. If you win, you get a point. If the computer wins, they get one point. If both pick the same element then no one gets a point. First person to 5 wins the game and is awarded with a victory point in the top left and right sides of the window.


To win see how elements beat one another:

Fire > Grass

Water > Fire

Grass > Water

Note: There is a bug with the win counters where the first win will still put up a 0. This will be addressed at a later date.
